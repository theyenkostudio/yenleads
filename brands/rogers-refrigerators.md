## Business Snapshot

* **Business Name:** Rogers Refrigeration.
* **Industry / Category:** HVAC/R (Heating, Ventilation, Air Conditioning, and Refrigeration).
* **Target Customer Type:** Residential (Homeowners) and Commercial Businesses.
* **Geographic Focus:** Based in Brandon, MS; serving Central Mississippi.
* **Experience:** Over 20 years of hands-on experience.

## Brand Personality

* **Brand Tone:** Reliable, professional, and community-oriented ("Mississippi's Best HVAC/R Company").
* **Perceived Trust Level:** High, emphasized by "honest, upfront pricing" and "top-notch workmanship".
* **Emotional Impression:** Calm and comfort-focused (evoked by imagery of a woman in a meditative pose under a running AC unit).
* **Values:** Reliability, Expertise, and Affordability.

## Visual Identity

* **Primary Visual Motif:** The White Magnolia flower (the state flower of Mississippi), used as a background for major text overlays.
* **Primary Colors:** * **White:** Used for primary headers and logo text for high visibility.
* **Natural Greens/Earth Tones:** Prominent in background imagery (plants, magnolias).


* **Typography Style:**
* **Logo/Main Headers:** Bold, all-caps Serif font (Classic and authoritative).
* **Subheaders:** Clean Sans-Serif with wide letter spacing (Modern and airy).
* **Body Text:** Simple, readable Sans-Serif.


* **Imagery:** Real-world team photos (technician with tools by a branded truck) mixed with lifestyle stock photography (indoor comfort, nature).

## Website Structure

* **Key Sections:** About Us, Services, Commercial Business, and Schedule Online.
* **Commercial Services:** Specific mention of manufacturer certifications and expertise with ice machines, coolers, and freezers.
* **Brands Serviced:** Includes Manitowoc, Hoshizaki, XLT Ovens, Heatcraft, Kolpak, Beverage Air, and True.

## UX & Conversion Observations

* **Conversion Points:** "Schedule Online" section with a dedicated email subscription field and a prominent phone number (**601-896-5747**).
* **Value Propositions:**
* 24/7 Support for emergency needs.
* Licensed team for both residential and commercial projects.


* **Visual Balance:** The site uses a "split-screen" layout in several areas, balancing text-heavy descriptions with relevant imagery.

## Key Gaps & Opportunities

* **Interactive Features:** While a subscription box exists, a more direct "Request a Quote" form could streamline lead generation.
* **Social Proof:** The visual design is strong, but adding customer testimonials directly to the homepage could further boost the "trusted expert" narrative.
* **Clarity on Refrigeration:** The "Commercial Business" section is very detailed, but the "Residential" side could benefit from similarly specific brand mentions (e.g., Trane, Rheem) to aid searchability.

## Unknowns / Assumptions to Avoid

* **Team Size:** While a technician is pictured, the total scale of the fleet and staff is not explicitly stated.
* **Financing:** It is unknown if they offer payment plans or financing for large system installs.
